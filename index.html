Who We Are

The Level X Group is a real estate firm comprised of 100+ combined years of experience across multiple industries & services areas

At LXG we take real estate assets with a core focus on hotels and apply our skills & resources to increase the value of the hotel assets which elevates the experience for the customer, and a win for our stakeholders.

Today our skills and resources have grown into 3 formal independent entities – Level Capital, Level Construction, and Level Hospitality – and work in unity to enable us the ability to provide this offering.
